What Got me into Computer Science

I have always regarded coding, hacking and cracking as something absolutely mesmerizing. Even when I did not properly know the technicalities involved in it, I was so enchanted by it that it all seemed like magic to me. Making me covet to become a some sort of 'magician’ in the future. Today, technology is a part of just about everything that we touch and use-right from the mobile phones we use, to the television we watch, to the way big multinational organizations function. Having a knowledge of different dimensions of computing is mandatory for every educated person now, so I believe. Moreover, an in-depth study of this subject enables a learner to solve deep, multidimensional problems requiring imagination, and this is exactly what I covet. It is the technology associated with computers that drive innovation in a host of other arenas such as engineering, business, entertainment and education.
