About Me

Hello! My name is James Henry and I am a sophomore chemical engineer at the University of Buffalo. I'm from Buffalo, NY, specifically Hamburg, which is roughly 15 minutes from the Bills stadium. Some hobbies I have are listening to music, such as Kendrick Lamar and Freddie Gibbs, playing rugby and browsing Tik-Tok. I'm apart of the Chi Beta Pledge class and have been enjoying the process of pledging Theta Tau!

Future Goals

  • Another goal I have is to not only grow in the academic field, but as a person as well. I believe as I get older, I change as a person, and I want to change in a way where I can be at my "best self". This goal is definitely long term, but it's one I care a lot about and really want to focus on.
  • Finally, I want to pursue a master's degree in the chemical field, specifically the nuclear side. I'm still not sure if that's the degree I want, but it's definitely a thought that has been on my mind for post-undergraduate.
